ABOUT ELIVATE9JA RAFFLES

Elivate9ja is a one-stop raffle, games and promotional platform available  for both online and physical participation.
It enables individuals, corporate, organisations and brands to host personalized prize competitions. 
Our platform is suitable for product promotions, customer rewards, charity events, fundraising drives and outdoor promotions. We provide the desired credibility for running raffles and distribution of  prizes to the winners through our digital selection software which turns everyday people’s dreams into reality.
In addition, as an impact driven organisation, we have committed a  percentage of our profits to support select charities across the country. 
Partnering with us has a tripple benefit effect:

  • Host all your personalised competitions in one place;
  • Transform the lives of everyday people; and
  • Improve the welfare of many more through our charities.

WHY SHOULD I PLAY WITH ELIVATE9JA RAFFLES

  • Raffling should be a source of light-hearted fun, and we are determined to keep it that way. We’ve got some great prizes. Whether you fancy taking the holiday of your dreams, getting behind the wheel of a brand new sports car, having the latest technology or embarking on a completely new experience, our raffles have got you covered. Our raffles are refreshed frequently to ensure you have variety. 
  • We have fantastic odds. Most online raffling websites charge crazy mark-ups on the real value of their prizes; these high mark-ups translate to more tickets, and more profit for the website operator, at the expense of the customer. With Elivate9ja, we have a different approach. We keep the number of entries low relative to the cost of the prize, and keep ticket prices low too, meaning that you get a truly fair experience, and ultimately a great chance of winning! 
  • We’re totally transparent. We disclose the number of tickets available for each raffle (unless it’s a charity raffle, where we want to raise as much money for the charity as possible), so you will always know exactly what your chances of winning is right from the outset. 
  • We will always keep ticket prices low. With Elivate9ja, you will never have to pay more than N500 to enter a raffle and secure your chance to win - we are committed to giving everyone a chance of winning, and even if you don’t win, you shouldn’t feel a dent in your bank account.
  • Even if you lose, you’ve helped someone win. We give 10% of every ticket purchased to charities, meaning that every ticket purchased on the website helps a good cause. So even if you don’t win the grand prize that you were hoping for, you contribute in creating happiness for others but we also know you will win. We also run raffles where majority of the funds from the tickets sold are donated directly to a particular charity.
